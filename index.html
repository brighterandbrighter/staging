About ADHD Coaching

  • Coaching is...

    • A collaboration between coach and client.
    • A non-judgmental conversation...
    • ...with someone who has a deep understanding of your unique brain wiring.
    • Focused on action, but a chance to process your emotions, too.
    • A place to come up with new strategies and make tough decisions.
    • An opportunity to learn to work better with the brain you have.

  • Coaching is not...

    • ...therapy. It's not about healing or "fixing" you; it's about taking action to get to where you want to be.
    • ...instruction. You won't be told what to do; we'll work together to come up with solutions that work for you (rather than the ones you've been told should work, but don't).
    • ...psychiatry. I can't diagnose you with ADHD, ASD, or anything else. However, I can signpost you to avenues for diagnosis if you wish, and some clients use coaching specifically to talk through the various pathways available.

How I work

...and how I can help

  • Adult 1:1 Coaching

    30 Minutes

    30-minute sessions can be helpful for clients working mainly on practical skills, or for those who do better focusing in short bursts.

    We will usually work together weekly for an initial period of around 12 weeks, but you can continue for as long as you find our coaching work helpful; we will review regularly throughout our work together to ensure you're getting the most out of the process.

  • Adult 1:1 Coaching

    60 Minutes

    60-minute sessions can be particularly helpful if you need the time and space to think through bigger life plans.

    They can also be useful if you are what we call a "verbal processor" - you need to really talk through your ideas to be sure of how you feel about them.

    We will usually work together weekly for around 12 weeks, but you can continue for as long as you find the process helpful. Some long-term clients change to fortnightly or monthly sessions for maintenance, while others pause when they've got all they need out of coaching. You are in the driving seat.

  • Workplace Consultancy

    On Request

    I offer bespoke workplace consultancy to employers and organisations interested in better understanding the needs of neurodivergent employees, reducing workplace stress, leading to higher productivity, and increasing staff wellbeing.

    Please contact me if you would like to discuss this, rather than booking a consultation directly.
